# KidsLearnAI Self-Financing Soft Launch Plan

## Executive Summary

This plan eliminates the need for upfront investment by generating revenue from day one using a 7-day trial model with required credit card. All growth is funded through reinvestment of profits.

---

## Part 1: Pricing Strategy

### Soft Launch Pricing (No Free Tier)

| Plan | Price | Billing |
|------|-------|---------|
| Monthly | €9.99/month | Recurring |
| Yearly | €99.99/year | Recurring (17% savings) |

### Trial Configuration
- **Duration:** 7 days
- **Credit Card:** Required upfront
- **Auto-convert:** Yes, to monthly plan
- **Cancellation:** Anytime during trial

### Why No Free Tier During Soft Launch
1. Attracts serious users who see value
2. Generates revenue immediately
3. Reduces support burden from tire-kickers
4. Creates urgency to evaluate the product
5. Can always add free tier later when profitable

## Part 2: Revenue Allocation Formula

### The 50/30/20 Rule

| Category | % | Purpose | Examples |
|----------|---|---------|----------|
| Growth Fund | 50% | Marketing & expansion | Content, tools, ads |
| Operations | 30% | Running costs | Servers, APIs, maintenance |
| Emergency | 20% | Safety buffer | Refunds, unexpected costs |

### Monthly Allocation Example

**At €300 MRR:**
- Growth: €150 (content writer, SEO tool)
- Operations: €90 (server costs, APIs)
- Emergency: €60 (saved for buffer)

**At €1,000 MRR:**
- Growth: €500 (VA, micro-influencers, ads)
- Operations: €300 (upgraded infrastructure)
- Emergency: €200 (3-month runway building)

## Part 5: Email Sequence for Trial Users

### Email 1: Welcome (Day 0, Immediate)
**Subject:** Welcome! Here's how to get started with [Child's Name]

**Content:**
- Warm welcome
- Quick start guide (3 steps)
- Recommend starting with AI Detective Game
- Set expectation for trial length

### Email 2: Feature Highlight (Day 2)
**Subject:** Have you tried the AI Friend Creator yet?

**Content:**
- Highlight a key feature
- Share what other kids love about it
- Soft reminder of trial benefits

### Email 3: Progress Check (Day 4)
**Subject:** How's [Child's Name] enjoying the games?

**Content:**
- Ask for feedback
- Offer help if stuck
- Share a tip for getting more value

### Email 4: Trial Ending Soon (Day 6)
**Subject:** Your trial ends tomorrow - here's what [Child's Name] will miss

**Content:**
- Recap benefits and progress
- List what's included in subscription
- Clear CTA to subscribe
- Mention yearly savings option

### Email 5: Final Day (Day 7)
**Subject:** Last chance to keep [Child's Name]'s progress

**Content:**
- Urgency without pressure
- Offer to help with any questions
- Simple subscribe button
- Thank them either way

### Email 6: Win-Back (Day 10, if not converted)
**Subject:** We saved [Child's Name]'s progress

**Content:**
- Let them know data is preserved
- Ask what held them back
- Offer to address concerns
- Soft re-engagement

## Part 8: Emergency Protocols

### Problem: Low Trial Signups (<5 per week)

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Review landing page messaging
2. Check signup flow for friction
3. Increase posting frequency
4. Try new channels
5. Ask existing users how they found you

### Problem: Low Trial Conversions (<15%)

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Survey users who didn't convert
2. Review onboarding experience
3. Add personal outreach (email/call offer)
4. Consider extending trial to 14 days
5. Add exit-intent offer

### Problem: High Churn (>15% monthly)

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Survey churned users
2. Add engagement features
3. Improve parent dashboard value
4. Send re-engagement campaigns
5. Consider loyalty/rewards program

### Problem: Refund Requests

**Protocol:**
1. Respond within 2 hours
2. Ask what went wrong (feedback)
3. Offer solution if possible
4. Process refund gracefully if requested
5. Log reason for product improvement
