## Self-Financing Pricing Strategy

### No Free Tier During Soft Launch
- Free tier reduces conversion pressure
- Instead: 7-day trial with credit card required
- Psychological commitment from day 1

### Irresistible Early Adopter Offer
**"Founding Member" Status**
- Pay $9.99/month now, keep this price forever
- When we raise to $19.99, you're locked in at $9.99
- First 100 customers only
- Creates urgency + rewards early support

### Alternative: Annual Upfront Payment
**"Pay for a year, get cash now"**
- Annual plan: $99 (paid upfront)
- Provides immediate cash flow
- Better than $9.99 Ã— 12 = $120 (16% discount)
- Use upfront cash to fund ads immediately

### Aggressive Conversion Tactics
- Email day 5 of trial: "2 days left!"
- Email day 6: "Tomorrow your trial ends"
- Email day 7: "Your trial is ending in 4 hours"
- Exit survey if they don't convert
- Offer: "Stay for $7.99 first month" (last chance)

## Risk Mitigation for Bootstrap Model

### Risk 1: Too Slow Growth
**Solution:**
- Increase price (test $14.99)
- Add B2B offering (sell to schools at $299/year per classroom)
- Offer consulting services alongside product

### Risk 2: High Churn
**Solution:**
- Email churned users within 24 hours
- Offer win-back discount: "Come back for $4.99/month"
- Improve onboarding based on feedback

### Risk 3: Running Out of Audience
**Solution:**
- Expand to adjacent markets (tutors, after-school programs)
- International expansion (starting with English-speaking countries)
- Add content in Spanish for Hispanic market

### Risk 4: Competitor Launches
**Solution:**
- Focus on customer relationships (small = nimble)
- Add unique features competitors can't copy quickly
- Build community (Facebook group, Discord)
